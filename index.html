The application uses finance APIs to generate a diverse range of stocks suitable for an individual’s stock preference. If a user is not aware of their risk tolerance, they have the option of participating in a quiz designed to determine their risk tolerance based on a set of carefully selected questions and a set algorithm. Once a risk tolerance is determined, the user is presented with a selection of stocks that align with their risk profile, as well as top news stories about the recommended stocks.
